A Journey into Sound

Frequency project designed to guide you through a journey of peace and introspection. This collection of sounds blends soothing ambient music with meditation music, crafting an atmosphere where sleep music and relaxing music become one. Let the calming music wash over you, as layers of healing and chillout music merge with nature sounds to create a serene escape from the noise of daily life.

Each track of "Exit and Rest" is a soundscape for meditation, a backdrop for yoga music, or the perfect study music to enhance focus in silence. With stress relief music at its core, this project invites you to relax, unwind, and find a moment of zen amidst the chaos. Whether you're seeking background music for a quiet evening, spa music for rejuvenation, or new age music for spiritual exploration, "Exit and Rest" offers a mysterious, yet comforting auditory experience.

Every visual and sound is original and produced by Exit and Rest.
